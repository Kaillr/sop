about us

Welcome to sop! We're a diverse gaming community based in Norway, originally founded in July 2022. While we are primarily based around osu!, we also have interests in all varieties of games.

At sop!, our goal is simple: to provide a welcoming space where gamers can connect, compete, and have a great time together. Whether you're a casual player or a sweat, there's a place for you here.

Join us on Discord to meet new friend, participate in our events, and be part of our growing community. From small events, to massive tournaments, there's always something exciting happening at sop!
